Iran

Iran, officially the Islamic Republic of Iran, is a country in Western Asia. With 82 million inhabitants, Iran is the world's 18th most populous country. It is the second-most populous Muslim-majority country (after Indonesia) and the ninth-most populous country in the world, covering a total area of 1,648,195 km2 (636,372 sq mi). Iran is bordered to the northwest by Armenia and the Republic of Azerbaijan, to the north by the Caspian Sea, to the northeast by Turkmenistan, to the east by Afghanistan and Pakistan, to the south by the Persian Gulf and the Gulf of Oman, and to the west by Turkey and Iraq. The country is separated from the rest of Asia by the Caucasus Mountains and the Alborz Mountains in the north, and the Zagros Mountains in the west. It also shares a short border with the Republic of Azerbaijan in the northwest at the Nakhchivan exclave.
